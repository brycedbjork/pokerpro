What is Poker Pro?

Poker Pro is a Computer Science final project intended to better educate users on the probability of poker.
Poker Pro consists of two main pages: Learn and Play
Learn is a training tool where you interactively choose a hand and discover the probability of getting certain five card combinations.
Play is a game where you have to estimate the probability of getting a certain combination given a starting hand of two cards.
